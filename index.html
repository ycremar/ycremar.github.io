Howdy!

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Texas A&M University, advised by Prof. Shuiwang Ji. My research work aims to improve the applicability and reliability of machine learning and deep learning approaches to drive broader industrial applications and scientific discovery (AI4Science). Specifically, my research focuses on theory-grounded self-supervised learning and spanning model explainability, graph neural networks, and their applications in drug discovery, quantum chemistry, biomedical imaging, and bioinformatics.
